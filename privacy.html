Privacy Policy

This application is used by authorized TikTok Shop sellers to manage and schedule product video publishing for their own TikTok accounts.

We may store the following information:

  • TikTok account authorization tokens
  • TikTok open_id and basic account identifiers
  • Granted OAuth scopes
  • Token expiration times
  • Video publishing metadata
  • Product IDs or product links
  • Scheduled publishing times
  • Publishing status and error logs

We use this information only to authenticate authorized TikTok accounts, schedule video publishing, publish selected videos through TikTok's official Content Posting API, and maintain publishing records.

We do not collect or store TikTok passwords.

We do not sell user data. We do not use TikTok account data for advertising, profiling, scraping, or unrelated purposes.

Users may revoke the application's access from their TikTok account settings at any time. After access is revoked, the application will no longer be able to publish videos for that TikTok account.

If a user requests deletion of stored authorization and publishing data, we will remove the associated tokens and publishing records from our system.
