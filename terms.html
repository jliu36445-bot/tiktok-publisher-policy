Terms of Service

This application is an internal publishing and scheduling tool for authorized TikTok Shop sellers.

The application allows authorized users to connect TikTok accounts through TikTok Login Kit and schedule seller-created product videos for publishing through TikTok's official Content Posting API.

Users must only connect TikTok accounts that they own or are authorized to manage. Users are responsible for ensuring that all videos, captions, product information, and publishing activity comply with TikTok's terms, community guidelines, advertising policies, and applicable laws.

The application does not collect TikTok passwords. TikTok account access is granted only through TikTok's official OAuth authorization flow.

The application does not scrape TikTok, automate engagement, send comments, send messages, follow accounts, or perform any action unrelated to authorized video publishing.

By using this application, users agree to use it only for lawful and authorized TikTok Shop product video management and publishing.
